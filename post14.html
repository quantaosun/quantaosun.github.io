Jie Jack Li name reaction book analysis

As his name is, so is his reaction, maybe

Posted by Quantao on December 31, 2020

It is with no doubt that the conception of "Name Reaction" seems quite alien to people without a chemistry background, the complex mechanisms and the form without any unification scared too many people even from chemistry society.

This blog will try to classify all the name reactions that have been covered by the Jie_Jack_Li Name Reaction published by Dr. Jie Jack Li in Springer Dordrecht Heidelberg London New York, in 2009, based on the reaction type or the name of the reaction. The purpose is to help general readers to have a different angle to access this fantastic book not only in alphabetic order.

All these tables are generated by a command to grab keywords from the book table of contents, it is not necessarily accurate in terms of defining what type of a reaction, but should be a good starting point for those who want to read this book.

Name reactions are always growing, some of them has been discovered a central ago like Friedel-Crafts reaction, which was discovered and optimized around 1887. Some others were discovered not very long ago, like Bartoli indole synthesis was developed around the 1980s, and professor Bartoli continues works on research about Bartoli reaction until today.
